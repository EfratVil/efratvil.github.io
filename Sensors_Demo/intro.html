Introduction


Robust Multivariate Statistics in Interactive Dashboards for - Remote Operations Centers.

The Precision Agriculture Demo


Problem: Transforming raw streaming sensors data into actionable dashboards
Solution: Data preprocessing with Multivariate statistical process control and robust statistics methods.

Visual interactive dashboards to:
  • Assist non statistics savvy operators to achieve situation awareness and take informed decisions.
  • Help analysts to decide on best models and fine tune parameters.
  • Create feedback loop Between operators and analysts.

This demonstrations contains visualizations used in the exploratory phase of the agriculture sensors use case.
