Methods for finding outliers

Different methods to find outliers:
T2 - Hotelling
PC10 - Euclidian distance of first 10 pc's
S - Correlation Matrix Sum (per sensor)
SS - Correlation Matrix Sum of Squares (per sensor)
CM - Attempt to join S & SS into one indicator.

Each one of the methods was performed 4 times. with different transformations applied to the sensor raw values.
