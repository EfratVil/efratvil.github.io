About

The Wikiprogress Data Visualization Contest 2015 was a good opportunity to learn and practice d3.JS.
The data used in the visualizations is from oecd.stats - Better Life Index for years 2013-2015. See the detailed definitions of the indicators.

The concept used: well being is relative between countries. In order to simplify the data visualization: each indicator values per country were reviewed and graded into 6 zones according to standard deviation distance from the mean. 2 more zones created for outlier data (extremely low or high).

Zones legend:
Worst      Below Avg Above Avg     Best 
0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
